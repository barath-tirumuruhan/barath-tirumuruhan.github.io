I am a 4th-year PhD candidate in Materials Science & Engineering at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities, advised by [Prof. Vivian Ferry](https://ferrygroup.umn.edu/). I model and optimize thin-film optoelectronic devices, primarily OLEDs, using Lumerical (FDTD & STACK) and MATLAB, working closely with experimental teams to guide design. My current project is industry-sponsored and under NDA, targeting next-generation OLED products.
